- (BOOL) beginFetchWithDelegate: (id)  delegate
didFinishSelector: (SEL)  finishedSEL
didFailSelector: (SEL)  networkFailedSEL 

Begin fetching the request.

|delegate| can optionally implement the two selectors |finishedSEL| and |networkFailedSEL| or pass nil for them. Returns YES if the fetch is initiated. Delegate is retained between the beginFetch call until after the finish/fail callbacks.
